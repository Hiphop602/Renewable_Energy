Introduction to Renewable Energy

What is Renewable Energy and why are they important?

Renewable energy is energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than they are consumed.
Sunlight and wind, for example, are such sources that are constantly being replenished.

 

Renewable energy sources are plentiful and all around us.
It reduces some types of air pollution drastically
They don't emit carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases that contribute to global warming. 
Renewable resources include biomass energy (such as ethanol), hydropower, geothermal power, wind energy, and solar energy.
